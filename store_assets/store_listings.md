**Description** (max 4,000 chars):  
Script is a beautifully designed, privacy-first, lightning-fast plain text and Markdown editor built for the modern creator.

It provides an unparalleled, distraction-free writing environment that seamlessly interacts with your local file system. From managing complex code syntax to intelligently formatting brain dumps using our integrated AI assistant, Script elevates your raw text workflow without compromising speed or local-first data integrity.

### ✨ Key Features

• **Local-First Privacy:** Your documents are saved natively on your device. No cloud servers scraping your data. Complete privacy, always.
• **Invisible Auto-Naming:** Just start typing. Script's AI will automatically read your content, generate a snappy title, pick a relevant emoji, and even assign the correct file extension in the background.
• **Smart Formatting Magic:** Brain dump your messy, unstructured thoughts. Click the magic wand button, and watch our AI instantly format it into beautiful, perfectly structured Markdown with headers and bullet points—without losing your original meaning.
• **Tone Adjuster:** Highlight any text and drag the AI slider to seamlessly shift your tone from blunt to highly diplomatic.
• **Instant Syntax Highlighting:** Paste code and watch it elegantly format on the fly with custom semantic coloring.
• **Kuscher Judgement:** Need brutal, honest feedback? Activate our signature AI Persona for an uncompromising critique of your writing.

Built cleanly on strict Material Design primitives, Script offers elegant dark mode transitions, polished typography scaling, and offline support. It is the ultimate lightweight editor for thinkers, developers, and writers.
